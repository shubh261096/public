We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information unless the app introduces optional features that require it. If such features are added, we will clearly communicate this within the app.

We may collect anonymous analytics information such as device type, app version, crash data, and general usage statistics.

If the app allows you to create or add content, it is stored locally on your device unless you choose to back it up using an external service.

We use the collected data to improve app performance, fix crashes, enhance features, and ensure a smooth user experience.
